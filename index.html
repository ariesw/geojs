Primarily developed by Kitware and Epidemico. GeoJS is intended to bridge the gap between GIS, Geospatial Visualization, Scientific Visualization and Infovis. GeoJS supports features such as Point, Line, Polygon, and advanced features such as Pixelmap, Contour, Heatmap, and Choropleth. GeoJS can read and render data from GeoJSON files, and WMS servers.
